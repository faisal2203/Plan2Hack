FAQs

What is the required team size?

The size of team can be 1 to 4 members

What is the scheduled date of the event ?

The event is scheduled on 12 May 2022.

What is meant by BVPIEEE

BVPIEEE is the student branch of IEEE at BVCOE. IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Is there any participation fee ?

There is no participation fee for the event.

On what basis will the initial screening take place ?

The initial screening will take place on the basis of the uniqueness of idea, feasibility, viability, and the tech stack used.

What is the eligibility criteria for participating in the event?

To participate in the event, all the team members must be enrolled in an undergraduate program..
